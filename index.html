Top three activities to do at Fontaine

Enjoy Fontaines under sea ecosystem

Fontaines waters, are homes to a vast array of sea life and coral structures that will have divers thinking they swam into another world.

Visit the Opera Epiclese

The Opera Epiclese is a great place to enjoy stunning plays and witness intense court dramas.

Enjoy the atmosphere of Vasari Passage

A keystone of Fontaines architecture, the Vasari Passage fountain acts as a social hub for all of fontaine.
